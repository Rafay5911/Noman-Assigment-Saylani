Movie

Set in a dystopian future where emotions are outlawed, "Silent Uprising" follows the journey of Elara, a young woman who begins to feel again after a mysterious encounter with a rebel group. As she navigates a society built on control and surveillance, Elara must choose between safety in silence and the dangerous power of emotion. With stunning visuals, a haunting score, and a deeply human core, this sci-fi thriller explores the cost of conformity and the unbreakable strength of the human spirit.
